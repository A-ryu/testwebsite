moments that make people smile all over the world through
the manufacture of automobile seats, convertible tops, railway
seats, welfare equipment, and automobile parts .

world and create the future of the next generation.

Management Philosophy

1. Putting Associate Satisfaction First

2. Shaping the Future with Human Warmth

Only when our associates are satisfied can we truly satisfy our customers. Dream-inspiring products flow from happy producers, and innovation comes from being free-spirited. Fair and impartial evaluation of each associate’s experience, ability, achievement level, motivation, and integrity will lead to job satisfaction, which in turn, will help the Company and its associates to grow in unison.

1.社員満足第一主義を貫く社員が満足してこそ、お客様に満足を届けられる。笑顔から夢のあるものは生まれ、自由闊達からイノベーションは起こる。経験・能力・目標達成度・やる気・人徳を適正・公平に評価することが働き甲斐につながり、社員と会社が成長する。


No matter how times change, there is always a need for maternal “warmth”. We aim to deliver products and services that bring smiles to the faces of our customers with such warmth. It is uniqueness that helps us to create value, gives us a competitive advantage and enables us to make a difference in the world.  A world full of life, in which young members of the community demonstrate their abilities through encouragement from their seniors while the elderly and the disadvantaged are supported and empowered-that is the kind of world that we envision. We hope to leave the world a better place and that children in the future will say. “Thank you for making our world so wonderful”

2.温かさと独自性で世界を笑顔にし、子供たちの未来を創造する 時代が変わろうとも必要なものは、お母さんのような「温かさ」。温かさでお客様を笑顔にできるような、製品・サービスを提供する 独自性からのみ価値を創造でき、競争優位に立て、世の中に貢献できる。若者が活躍し、それを先輩がサポートする。高齢者・挑戦者をサポートし、元気にする。そういう活き活きした世の中にする。子供たちから、「いい世の中にしてくれて
ありがとう」と言われる最後でありたい。
